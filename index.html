Where Does It Hurt?

An interactive website to figure out why you are feeling pain and where it could possibly come from!

Welcome to the "Where Does It Hurt?" website! Please note that this is a simple interactive tool and not a substitute for professional medical advice. If you believe your condition is serious or dangerous, do not hesitate to seek professional medical help. Another warning, this is just made by a simple teenager, so if there are some inaccuracies or mistakes, please do not be afraid to point it out! Constructive criticism is always welcomed. Enjoy!

To get started, simply click on the "Diagnosis" tab in the menu above. You will answer some questions to get some basic understanding of your symptoms, and the diagnosis will be provided. Note that it may not be completely accurate, so please consult with a licensed healthcare expert or professional for a serious diagnosis. Have fun!
